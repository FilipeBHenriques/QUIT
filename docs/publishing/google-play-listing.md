# Google Play Listing

## App Title

QUIT: App & Website Blocker

## Short Description

Block distracting apps and websites with a fast, focus-first blocker.

## Full Description

QUIT helps you reduce distraction by blocking the apps and websites that pull you off track.

Choose the apps you want to avoid, add the websites you want to limit, and QUIT steps in with a blocking screen when you try to open them. The goal is simple: create friction at the moment of temptation so you can pause and make a better next choice.

Key features:

- Block selected apps on your Android device
- Block selected websites in supported browsers
- Show a fast full-screen blocker when blocked content is opened
- Manage blocked apps and blocked websites in one place
- Track progress and in-app stats
- Set time-based limits for blocked app use

Why people use QUIT:

- Reduce compulsive scrolling
- Stay focused during work or study
- Limit access to high-distraction apps and websites
- Build better habits by interrupting automatic behavior

How it works:

- You choose which apps and websites to block
- QUIT detects foreground app changes and supported browser visits on your device
- When blocked content is detected, QUIT shows a blocking screen instead of letting you continue without friction

Required permissions:

QUIT requires Accessibility Service, Usage Access, and Display Over Other Apps to provide its core blocking features.

- Accessibility Service is used to help detect supported browser website visits and foreground transitions needed for blocking
- Usage Access is used to detect foreground app usage and support blocking and time tracking
- Display Over Other Apps is used to show the blocking screen above blocked content

These permissions are only used to provide the blocking features that you explicitly enable.

Notes:

- QUIT is designed for personal focus and self-control use
- Website blocking support depends on browser behavior and accessibility-readable address bars
- Some Android devices may require battery optimization exclusions for reliable background operation
